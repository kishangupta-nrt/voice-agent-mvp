# Company FAQ - Customer Service

## General Questions

Q: What are your business hours?
A: We're open Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM. We're closed on weekends and major holidays.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach us by calling our main line, using the chat feature on our website, or sending an email. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Q: What is your refund policy?
A: We offer full refunds within 30 days of purchase for unused items. For damaged items, we offer replacements or store credit. Just contact us with your order number.

## Order Related

Q: How do I track my order?
A: You can track your order using the tracking number in your confirmation email. Visit our website and enter the number in the tracking box, or call us with your order number.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. International shipping takes 10-14 business days.

Q: Can I change my order after placing it?
A: Yes, you can modify your order within 24 hours of placing it. Contact us with your order number and tell us what you'd like to change.

Q: What if my package is lost?
A: If your package hasn't arrived within the expected timeframe, contact us. We'll work with the shipping carrier to locate it or send a replacement.

## Account & Billing

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your email and we'll send you a link to reset it.

Q: How do I update my payment information?
A: Log into your account, go to Settings, then Payment Methods. You can add, remove, or update payment methods there.

Q: Where can I find my invoices?
A: All your invoices are available in your account under Order History. You can also request invoices via email.
